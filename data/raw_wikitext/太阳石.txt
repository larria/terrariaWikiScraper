'''{{tr|Sun Stone}}'''是一种[[{{tr|Hardmode}}]][[{{tr|Golem}}]]后的[[{{tr|accessory}}]]，有 {{eicons|1.4.0.1}} {{chance|1/7}} / {{eicons|1.4.0.1|invert=y}} {{chance|1/8}} 的几率掉落于{{tr|Golem}}。它提供和[[{{tr|Moon Stone}}]]相同的属性奖励，但这些{{tr|bonuses}}仅在[[{{tr|day}}]]生效。

属性奖励如下：{{source code ref|v=1.4.3.6|method=Terraria.Player.UpdateEquips}}
* +10% {{tr|melee speed}}
* +10% [[{{tr|damage}}]] (全部类型)
* +2% [[{{tr|critical strike}}]]几率 <!-- magic, melee, and ranged, there's no such thing as "minion critical strike chance" -->
* +1 HP/s [[{{tr|health regeneration}}]]<!-- Note that in the code, it says "lifeRegen += 2", but each point of the "lifeRegen" equals to 0.5 HP/s. -->
* +4 [[{{tr|defense}}]]
* +15% [[{{tr|mining speed}}]]
* +0.5 [[{{tr|summon}}]][[{{tr|knockback}}]]
