'''{{tr|Confetti Gun}}'''是一种消耗性的{{tr|novelty weapon}}，可发射一大堆不造成任何{{tr|damage}}的缤纷{{tr|confetti}}。它的效果是纯粹美学性的，并没有实际用途。{{tr|Confetti Guns}}是一次性用品，击发完后即刻消失。它们可以以每个 {{iteminfo|Confetti Gun|buy}} 的价格从[[{{tr|Party Girl}}]]处购买。

== 备注 ==
* 虽然它视觉上发射的是{{tr|confetti}}，但{{tr|Confetti Gun}}实际上跟[[{{tr|Confetti}}]]（一种[[{{tr|crafting material}}]]）无关。
* {{tr|Confetti Gun}}并非是一把真正消耗[[{{tr|ammunition}}]]的[[{{tr|gun}}]]。它其实更像是类似于{{wikipedia|party popper|礼花炮}}的一次性用品。
* {{tr|Confetti Gun}}在内部被视为是[[{{tr|ranged}}]]伤害{{tr|item}}，因此受益于[[{{tr|ammo conservation}}]]。但是，仅限于[[{{tr|armor}}]]{{tr|items}}所提供的{{tr|ammo conservation}}奖励，[[{{tr|Magic Quiver}}]]、[[{{tr|Ammo Box}}]]和[[{{tr|Ammo Reservation Potion}}]]对{{tr|Confetti Gun}}不起作用。{{source code ref|v=1.4.4.9|method=Terraria.Player.ItemCheck}}<!-- The usual, "actual" ammo conservation check in Terraria.Player.PickAmmo() that includes the Ammo Box and other items is skipped because that method is only called for items with useAmmo > 0, which is not the case for the Confetti Gun. -->
